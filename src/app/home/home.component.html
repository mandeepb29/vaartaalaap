About Us

The world of conversation oscillates through problems of bias, length, medium and so on. As a founder of Vaartaalaap, I have been a viewer of news, debates, interviews in various forms on digital and non-digital channels. Where conversations have meandered aimlessly at times as well as been unable to promote the voice of a common consumer.

Vaartaalaap is an Indian startup, creating digital conversational content for global audiences. As well as encouraging consumers to generate their own conversational content. Creating voice for common people.

With a growth of over 15% (Compound Annual Growth Rate) in conversational content. Vaartaalaap is making a beginning as well.

Vision

Vaartaalaap wishes to be the voice of common people through its digital presence. Where subscribers and listeners get enthused with honest conversations; related to topics in vogue or of common good in an unbiased way.

Consumers get to search, create their own as well as promote their own creativity through user generated content. The platform would also enrich and provide a compelling customer experience for all stakeholders. As part of its global consciousness, the platform will promote humane values towards all, whilst promoting empowerment and climate goals of communities globally in next 10 years.

Mission

Vaartaalaap’s mission is to provide a platform for earnest interactions with commoners and celebrities in the form of interviews, debates and discussions. Along with promoting common people to interview others in short focused videos about topics in vogue.
